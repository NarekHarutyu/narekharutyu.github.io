Narek Harutyunyan

Robotics Researcher | Reinforcement Learning | Autonomous Systems | Multi-Robot Systems

Narek Harutyunyan is a rising senior at Brown University, concentrating in Computer Engineering. His research focuses on robotics and artificial intelligence, with an emphasis on reinforcement learning, machine learning, stability guarantees in locomotion, and multi-robot systems. Narek has contributed to cutting-edge research projects at Caltech ARCL Lab, Carnegie Mellon University AirLab, and Brown University ACT Lab, working on advancing reliable and adaptive robotic systems including autonomous drones, quadruped robots, and AI-driven exploration systems.
