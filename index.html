Narek Harutyunyan

Robotics Researcher | Reinforcement Learning | Autonomous Systems | Multi-Robot Systems

I'm a senior at Brown University majoring in Computer Engineering. My research focuses on robotics and artificial intelligence, particularly reinforcement learning, stability in locomotion, and multi-robot systems. I've worked on advancing reliable and adaptive robotic systems—including autonomous drones, quadruped robots, and AI-driven exploration platforms—through research at Caltech's ARCL Lab, Carnegie Mellon's AirLab, and Brown's ACT Lab.
